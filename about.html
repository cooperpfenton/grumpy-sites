About Me

The person behind Grumpy Sites.

Hey, I'm Cooper.

I started Grumpy Sites because I was shocked by the high prices web agencies quoted for websites.

All throughout my schooling, I found it hard to sit still, get good grades, finish work on time, and pay attention. However, I found it entertaining and fun to play around on computers, building powerpoints, etc. Thats when I found out I had low functioning ADHD. I always thought I was just lazy, or that I picked up things slower than others. But one thing caught my attention, and that was building things on my computer.

Being the tall skinny kid, I was looked down upon when it came to sports, and when it came to things at workplaces, like moving equipment. Thankfully theres none of that in building on computers, so you can depend on me in that aspect.

I love making a difference and seeing smiles on faces, thats why I want to try my hardest to build the best websites for you and your company. :)

Let's work together.

Got a project in mind? I'd love to hear about it.
